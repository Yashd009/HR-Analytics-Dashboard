#  HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI Project)

##  Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze employee data, track attrition trends, and evaluate workforce performance.

This dashboard helps HR teams and management make data-driven decisions by providing insights into employee demographics, retention, performance, and salary distribution.

---

##  Key Objectives

* Analyze **employee attrition patterns**
* Monitor **workforce demographics**
* Evaluate **department-wise performance**
* Understand **salary vs performance relationship**
* Improve **employee retention strategies**

---

##  Dashboard Preview

###  Employee Demographics & Attrition Analysis

* Total Employees: **1.47K**
* Total Attrition: **237**
* Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
* Average Age: **37**

**Insights:**

* Highest employees in age group **26–35**
* Sales department has the **highest attrition rate (~20.63%)**
* Employees with lower salary range show **higher attrition**

---

###  Performance & Salary Analysis

* Employee Retention Rate: **83.88%**
* High Performing Employees: **15.37%**
* Avg Training Hours: **2.80 hrs**
* Gender Ratio (M:F): **1.50**

**Insights:**

* Sales department has **highest average salary (~7K)**
* Performance ratings are relatively stable across departments
* Overall employee satisfaction score: **2.73 / 4**

---

##  Key Features

*  Interactive filters (Department, Gender, Job Role, Salary Slab, Age Group)
*  Dynamic KPIs and cards
*  Drill-down insights using slicers
*  Visualizations include:

  * Bar Charts
  * Pie Charts
  * Line Charts
  * Scatter Plot
  * KPI Cards
  * Gauge Chart

---

##  Dataset Information

The dataset includes the following key fields:

* Employee ID
* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Salary
* Attrition (Yes/No)
* Performance Rating
* Training Hours
* Years at Company
